Programs

Our programs are designed to help kids flourish

PRESCHOOL PROGRAM

2.5 years -3 year olds (Caterpillars)

​This class introduces letters, numbers, colors and shapes to the children. Lesson plans include language development, learning the alphabet, recognizing numbers and learning to count, music and movement, creative art, story time. The center use blocks, puzzles, creative art, and books. At this stage, toddlers are becoming more independent and our teachers promote practicing life skills like hand washing, picking up toys and sharing with friends. Children are introduced to cognitive skills and practice making decisions. Science and discovery activities stimulate curiosity as the children learn valuable lessons about themselves and the world around them. Art and music activities will be sued to help the children develop individual expressions.

PRESCHOOL PROGRAM

3- 4 year olds (Butterflies)

In this class the focus is placed on language arts and math fundamentals to promote excitement about learning new concepts. For these active learners our lesson plans are filled with activities to help each child grow socially, intellectually and physically. Language development includes story time and puppet play to enrich their imaginations and teach listening and verbal interaction in the group setting. The children will start to write short sentences as well as words. In math, they will go beyond just counting and learn to group and recognize patterns.

PRE KINDERGARTEN

4 -5 Year olds (Eagles)

This class is considered a Kindergarten Prep Class. It promotes cooperative play to learn problem solving through lesson plans that teach the children to use independent reasoning and develop manners and social skills. Our teachers introduce the structure of a classroom setting and concentrate on early reading and math skills. Language lessons include story time followed by content questions. Phonics and writing are stressed. In math, adding and subtracting are introduced along with graphing, grouping, sorting and comparisons. In Science and Discovery, they will learn about animals, the earth and outer space, the weather, magnets and magnifying glasses, and the five senses. Social studies teach the children about their community and different cultures.

CHILD SAFTEY

Grow and Blossom Preschool Center is committed in providing a secure and safe environment for your child as well as peace of mind for the family. Entrances to the program are controlled by codes issued only to families and staff. A surveillance camera will also monitor the center's interior and exterior. Children are under the constant supervision of staff that is recognized as the first and most effective source of our security.

MEALS

A nutritious breakfast, lunch, mid-afternoon snack are served each day. Monthly menus are posted on bulletin boards and will be left in your child's cubby. If a child requires a special diet due to food allergies, a doctor’s written statement is needed, along with a list of allowed and prohibited foods. We will make every effort to accommodate religious and cultural dietary requirements. Please discuss your child’s needs with the administrative staff. Meals are served family style in the classroom at a common table and provide rich opportunities for learning and socializing.

HOLIDAY AND TRAINING CALENDAR

Grow and Blossom Preschool Center is closed for selected holidays and three days of in-service training each year. The training days are scheduled within the year to coincide with public school’s staff development days and specific dates will be posted and families informed with sufficient time to make alternative arrangements.

WEATHER AND EMERGENCY RELATED CLOSINGS

Grow and Blossom Preschool Center will follow the Federal Government schedule for any weather and emergency closings. If conditions or events require closing during a day of operations, parents will be contacted directly; however, children will remain in care under supervision in a safe setting until picked up by an authorized adult. In the event that the center must be evacuated due to an environmental or building emergency, teachers and children will be transported to Calvary Road Baptist Church at 6811 Beulah St, Alexandria, VA 22310.
